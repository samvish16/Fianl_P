Addendum: Creative Process

The "Lights Out" game was designed with a focus on creating an engaging and visually appealing experience for players. The game board is structured with a sleek grid layout, bordered and shadowed to give it a modern look.

Stylistically, contrasting colors were used to enhance visibility and create a sense of interaction. The transition effect on cell clicks adds a subtle but satisfying visual feedback to the player's actions.

One challenge faced during development was ensuring random yet solvable starting configurations for the board. This was overcome by implementing a toggle function that simulates clicks, allowing for a white board that becomes solvable upon user interaction.

Overall, the iterative design process and attention to detail resulted in a polished game interface that delivers an enjoyable gaming experience while maintaining simplicity and elegance.
